Website Ideas

Learn more about why I picked these ideas

Personal Blog

A personal blog would describe day-to-day personal updates. I would include
things like what I did during the day, photos of my activities, as well as
photos of my friends. I'm with my friends frequently throughout the day, so it
would be a nice way to document the things that we do together. I could also
share it with my friends to view! It's like a digital scrapbook. The tone would
be fun and playful.

Art Portfolio

An art portfolio would serve as a hub for my artwork. I can upload works as I
create them and have a visual history of my progress all in one place. It
would also serve as a good way to display my work for others to view it. The
tone would be professional, but fun enough to show my personality as it
applys to my art.

Cat Blog

A cat blog would focus on my cat, William. It would consist mainly of photos
of him as he exists throughout the day. Like the personal blog, this would be
something like a digital scrapbook. The tone would be playful and happy.
