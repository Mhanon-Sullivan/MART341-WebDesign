Why I Picked These Website Ideas

Personal Blog

I thought to do a personal blog because I really love documenting what my
friends and I do together. I've been dubbed as the photographer of the group,
and I'm constantly being asked to send pictures to our group chat. A good way
to remedy this is with a website that my friends can go to and pull pictures
from directly. It's also a great way to document our friendship and my day-to-day
life.

Art Portfolio

I thought to do an art portfolio because I need a storage system for my art that
is both aesthetically pleasing and easily-accessible by others. I want people to
see my work. As an animator, I need a place for employers to go to see what I
can do, and a portfolio page would do excactly that.

Cat Blog

I thought to do a cat blog because I want to create a storage system of photos
of my cat, William. I have hundreds of photos and would love to organize
them. It's like his own personal section of a scrapbook, except the whole
scrapbook is his (as it should be).
